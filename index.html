Skills

User Research

My user research is centered around gaining deep insights into the needs, behaviors, and preferences of the target audience. Approaching user empathy is a crucial aspect of user-centered design. I follow a systematic and empathetic process to ensure a comprehensive understanding of users, which ultimately informs good product design and development decisions.

Methodologies: User interviews & surveys, usability testing, competitor analysis, data analysis

My approach to user interface design is to create visually appealing, intuitive, and fun user-centric interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. iterative process that empowers creativity and problem-solving.
